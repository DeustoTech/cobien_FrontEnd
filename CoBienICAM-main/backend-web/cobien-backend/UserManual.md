# User Manual — CoBien Web

> Practical guide for users of the CoBien web platform.

---

## 1. What is CoBien?

CoBien is a website for **communication and organization** between a web user and an associated **device** ("mueble"). From the website you can:

* Send **text and image** messages to your device (the **Pizarra** app).
* View and filter **events** in a **calendar** (global and personal).
* Receive **notifications** from the device (e.g., "ready for video call") and manage them.
* Start a **video call** from the website.

> Note: Some options may vary depending on your account’s permissions or configuration.

---

## 2. Requirements

* An up-to-date **browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.
* A stable **Internet connection**.
* A **CoBien account** (username and password).

---

## 3. Access and navigation

### 3.1 Sign up (Register)

1. Click **Sign Up**.
2. Fill out the form.
3. Go to the email you provided to confirm the verification.
4. You’ll be redirected to the login page.

### 3.2 Log in

1. Open the CoBien URL.
2. Enter your **username** and **password**.
3. Click **Log in**.

> If you forgot your password, use the **Forgot your password?** link.

### 3.3 Top menu

* **Events**: opens the calendar.
* **Video calls**: access the call flow (if enabled for your account).
* **Pizarra**: messaging with your device.
* **Language selector (ES/FR)**: change the interface language.

### 3.4 Log out

* Use the user menu (top right) → **Log out**.

---

## 4. Pizarra (messaging)

Pizarra lets you send messages (text and/or image) to your device and view the **history** by recipient.

### 4.1 Screen areas

* **Recipient selector**: chips and/or a field with autocomplete to choose who you’re writing to.
* **New message**: text area + button to attach an image (with preview).
* **History**: list of sent messages (shows a thumbnail if the message includes an image).
* **Notifications** (sidebar): inbox with an unread counter.

### 4.2 Send a message

1. Choose a **recipient** (or type it if it doesn’t appear).
2. Enter your **text**.
3. *(Optional)* Attach an **image** (maximum size **5 MB**).
4. Click **Send**. You’ll see the message in the **history**.

> Tip: if you attach an image, wait for the **preview** to appear before sending.

### 4.3 View and filter history

* When you select a recipient, messages with that person/device are loaded.
* Scroll to see earlier messages. (If pagination is available, use **Load more**.)

### 4.4 Device notifications

* The side panel shows **incoming notifications** (e.g., "Available for call").
* Available actions:

  * **Mark as read** (single).
  * **Mark all**.
  * **Go to video call** (when applicable).

---

## 5. Video calls (optional)

If you are logged in, you can access the **Video calls** button.

### 5.1 Start a video call

1. From **Pizarra**, use the CTA in the notifications panel **or** go to **Video calls** from the top menu.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions.

> Requirements: allow **microphone** and **camera** in your browser when prompted.

---

## 6. Events calendar

The calendar shows **global events** (visible to everyone) and **personal events** (targeted to you or your contacts).

### 6.1 View filters

* **All**: shows both global and personal events.
* **Global**: only public/global events.
* **Personal**: you’ll see a **dropdown** with people/labels relevant to you.

### 6.2 View an event’s details

* Click the event to open its **details** (date, time, description, etc.).

### 6.3 Create/edit events (if your account has permission)

1. Click **New event**.
2. Fill in the required fields.
3. Choose **who should see** this event.
4. Save.

> If you don’t see this option, your account may not have edit permissions.

---

## 7. Languages (ES/FR)

* Change the language from the **selector** in the top menu.
* The content and interface will update automatically.

---

## 8. Tips

* **Images**: use common formats (JPG/PNG). Keep the size under **5 MB**.
* **Privacy**: avoid sharing sensitive data in messages or event descriptions.
* **Accessibility**: you can increase your browser’s zoom (Ctrl/Cmd + "+") for better readability.

---

## 9. Troubleshooting (FAQ)

**I’m not receiving notifications.**
• Check your connection.
• Reload the page.
• See whether they are already **marked as read** or may have expired.

**I can’t attach an image.**
• Check the format and make sure it’s under **5 MB**.
• Re-select the file.

**I can’t start the video call.**
• Make sure you allow **microphone** and **camera** in the browser.
• Close other apps using the camera.
• Try a different browser.

**I don’t see the option to create events.**
• Your account may not have edit permissions.

**The site isn’t loading properly.**
• Clear your browser cache and try again.
• Try Incognito/Private mode to rule out extensions.
